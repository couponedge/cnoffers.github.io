Coding Ninjas vs GeeksForGeeks DSA Course Comparison & Review

September 14, 2021

Short Answer:



Coding Ninjas is best for anyone new to programming or beginners as Coding Ninjas focuses on teaching problem solving and in-depth concept explanations.

Whereas GeeksForGeeks is best for someone preparing for the interview and wants short explanations on standard questions asked in an interview. It doesn't focus on problem-solving. Instead, it more focuses on teaching concepts.

With my experience having done courses from both sites, I will recommend doing courses from Coding Ninjas as I found it more interactive, better teaching style, better doubt support. Coding Ninjas is a bit expensive compared to GeeksforGeeks, but it boils down to the quality of content.

Coding Ninjas has the best quality content.

Quality:


  • I found coding ninjas has better quality lectures. As a comparison, you can have a look at these videos. (Coding Ninjas Video, GeeksForGeeks Video)
  • Ankush Singla sir, an instructor at coding ninjas, looks more confident while teaching and gives us a better impression. 
  • Coding Ninjas Teaching style is better than GeeksforGeeks. 
  • Code is written in desktop and explained each line while writing why we are writing this line of code. While in the case of GeeksForGeeks, code is written directly on the whiteboard without even explaining how each line is thought before writing. After the code is written, the code explanation is given. This is a bit weird for me. It would have been better if code was shown writing on desktop.

Assignments:


  • Both sides provide us with assignments to practice.
  • But in the case of geeksforgeeks, videos are given in one section and assignments are given in another. Which makes it a bit difficult to watch all the videos completely first, then do the assignments, or watch one video and then solve one problem.
  • But everything is given in a sequence order in coding ninjas. They have 1-2 videos first explaining the concept. And then problems based on the concept and some MCQs.

Hint Videos:


  • Coding Ninjas provides hint videos in most of the assignment problems. Which helps us in understanding how to approach the problem and re-enforce our problem-solving skills.
  • But in the case of GeeksforGeeks, there are no hint videos. And you need to figure it out how to solve the problem, which doesn't help in increasing problem solving skills.

Instructors:


  • Coding Ninjas course has Ankush Singla as an instructor, who had completed his B.Tech from IIT-D and masters from Stanford University.
  • GeeksForGeeks instructor is Sandeep Jain, who had done their masters from IIT-Roorkee.

College doesn't matters here, what matters is whose teaching style you are able to understand.


UI/UX:


  • GeeksForGeeks UI is very basic, no modern UI elements are used. Bit unproductive UI I would say, DOubt system can be embedded with problems. And videos lectures UI can also be improved.
  • Coding Ninajs provided fully modern UI design. Which is very impressive to use, which also increases productivity and saves time.

UI doesn't affect our learning experience directly, but I believe it indirectly affect our learning experience and mood.


Doubt Support:


  • Coding ninjas has the best doubt support in online courses. You can chat with TA from the problems page itself. TA can also debug code also and help your via chat, audio/video calls if required.
  • GeeksForGeeks also have a doubt support, but its not so smooth as Coding Ninjas. You have to navigate to other tab to ask doubt. And you have to manually send codes for debugging.

Placements:


Both Websites provides placement support to the students after completing the course from their website.


Assignment Access:

  • Assignments can be access for lifetime in both websites.
  • Video in coding ninjas are available only for the course duration, after the course ends. You can't watch the videos, but you can access hint videos and assignment for lifetime.
  • However in GeeksforGeeks videos and assignments are available for lifetime.

Price


  • GeeksforGeeks course are less expansive and cost around Rs 4000.
  • Coding Ninjas course cost 2-3 times more than GeeksforGeeks. 
  • The difference in price is due to the different in quality both websites provides.

Discounts on courses

Free Trail


  • Coding Ninjas provides free trail of their course where you can learn 2-3 topics and then decide whether you should buy the course or not?
  • GeeksforGeeks doesn't; provide free trail of their course, they only provide 1 demo video which I feel is not sufficient to decide the overall course and make a purchase.

Refund Policy


  • If in any case you don't want to continue the course, you can ask for refund in coding ninjas within 7 days and get your money back.
  • but in case of geeksforgeeks there is no refund policy, Once you purchase the course you can't take the money back.

My Experience:


  • I felt coding ninjas provides more in-depth explanations and explains the concepts in a better way. Whereas geeksforgeeks is good for revision but not good for students just starting to learn data structures.
  • Video quality and presentation of coding ninjas are better than that of geeksforgeeks.
  • Coding ninjas focuses more on problem solving and how to convert the idea into code, whereas geeksforgeeks focuses mostly on explaining the concepts and then explaining the code.
  • You can compare both platform with an example of android and iPhone. Both has almost similar functionality but one costs more than other. similar is the case with geeksforgeeks and coding ninjas, coding ninjas cost more due to its quality.
  • If I had to rate both platforms. I would rate 9/10 to coding ninjas, and 5/10 to GeeksforGeeks.


At the end, It's your call which platform you should choose to learn data structures.
I just gave an high-level overview of what I felt after doing both courses.

You can always check free trail or demo videos of both platforms and decide which suit your needs better. But I would recommend you to giving coding ninjas a try.
