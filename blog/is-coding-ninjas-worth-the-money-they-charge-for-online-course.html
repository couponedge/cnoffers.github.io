Is Coding Ninja Institute worth the money they charge for an online programming course?
October 01, 2020

In 1 word “YES” it is worth the money they charge.

Let me explain in-depth how it is worth.

 

Firstly understand that Coding Ninjas don’t just charge for their videos there are many other benefits you get along it which separates it from other similar sites which provides similar courses at low price.

 

Let me explain it with my own experience:

I joined coding ninjas competitive programming course when I was at std 12 because I wanted to participate in IOI which is International Olymipads in Informatics. It has same syllabus as competitive programming. And That time I only knew some basic Data Structures. And could not find anyone to guide me for it. Hence I searched online and get to know about coding ninjas course.

 

After explaining my parents why I needed that course, my parents allowed me to purchase the course which cost me around Rs 10000/-. At that time I didn’t knew much and struggled a lot in problem solving but with time improved a lot.

 

Teaching Assistance : During the course whenever I have doubt regardless of how silly doubt it was, I can always raise a doubt and get response within 10 minutes to 1 hour mostly.

 

 

Content : The best thing I liked about coding ninjas was I don’t had to google around for different topics, All the topics are already covered with enough problems to practice, sorted in increasing difficulty order which makes learning so easy.

 

 

Result : After completing I gave my ZCO (zonal computing olympiads) exam and got selected for the Next Level.

 

 

 

Improvement in competitive sites: After the course i was now able to solve medium level problems and improve my ranking, before the course I used to stuck after solving 1st two problems but now I can solve more problems.

 

 

Problem Setting: Apart from this best part till now was I had also set few problems on codechef contest now.

 

Moreover, after the completion of the course, Coding Ninjas provides Internship and Job opportunities in various companies including Coding Ninjas itself. I applied for Teaching Assistant Internship in Coding Ninjas after the completion of my course.

 

 

The selection was based on a Coding Test which I passed easily and got selected as a Teaching Assistant. My role is to help other students in code/concepts in the course which I completed recently. So, this Internship provided me complete revision of the course. It is a paid internship and providing me a stipend of 30k.

 

 

So, overall I invested 10k in the course and in return I learnt a lot and got an internship of 30k. Hence, it proved to be a value for money deal !

 

Therefore, if you are interested in any of the course provided by Coding Ninjas, don’t think much…. Just go for it and trust me .. you won’t regret on your decision !!

 

It is an investment for upskilling yourself !!

I am telling you in brief all those things about the course, which I personally liked the most and I think that you should also know these things -

Placement Assistance - After completing the course, Coding Ninjas ensures that you should get the right track in future. It provides various internships and jobs opportunities with a good stipend. With the help of these, you can get back your course fees within a month. So, I consider it just like an investment !!


Teaching Assistance - If in case you have any problem or doubt regarding any topic, concept, question or anything, you can ask in from the teaching assistant. They explain you the solution very clearly. You may ask them any doubt as many time as you want. You will definitely get the solution within few hours.


CODEZEN - CODEZEN is an online platform specially made for all the students of Coding Ninjas to practice. It contains various sections like blogs, test section, events section, leaderboards etc. It allows all the students of Coding Ninjas to compete among themselves.


Refer & Earn Scheme - I personally loved this scheme a lot. After taking the course, you can refer other students to take Coding Ninjas courses. You can provide them 1000 rupees additional discount and you also get 1000 rupees in your wallet which you can use to buy further courses.
