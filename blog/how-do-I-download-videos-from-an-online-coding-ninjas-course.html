How do I download videos from an online Coding Ninjas course?
October 03, 2020

Downloading lecture videos is not possible in Coding Ninjas website, because the content videos have been encrypted and it's also DRM protected. But It can be done via Coding Ninjas Mobile App.


Downloading videos of the course allows you to watch the lectures anytime without internet & without buffering. You can easily download any video of your Coding Ninjas online course by following these steps-


  • First you need install Coding Ninjas android application in your mobile phone. It is available for free on Google Play Store.
  • Then you need to login in the app with your that account from which you have taken the course.
  • Then simply select the lecture you want to download. Suppose I choose the first lecture, “Introduction to Recursion”.
  • Then on opening the lecture, you will see an option to download the video.


  • Then simply select the quality and download the lecture.


  • After the completion of the download, you can see downloaded icon with the video and now you can access it any time without internet ;)



The advantage of downloading the lectures is that you can watch them offline also. But you can access these videos inside the app only! This download is not permanent and you can watch it only till your course duration.


Moreover, downloading course related videos from Coding Ninjas website is not possible. The content videos are highly encrypted and its also DRM protected. Trying to download content with unethical ways is prohibited.


So, I will suggest you not to try downloading the content because if you are caught trying to download by the authorities then your account may be suspended and the payment you made for the course will not be refunded.


“Remember that someone is trying to create that valuable content for you while working for days don't try to steal their work”.


Also Don’t Try to screen record the videos because your email is will floating in the window and changes it position every 4-5sec so you will be caught if found doing this, and the result of this can be very dangerous.
